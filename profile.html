About Jane Willow

Jane Willow (born 31st May 1074) is a Victorian Guard of Honour who gained public attention through her appearance in the Victorian Army recruitment campaign featured in ALBION AFFAIRS magazine (Issue #47, Spring 1096).

Known for her striking blonde hair and elegant appearance, Willow became the face of Victorian military recruitment between 1095–1097.

Little is known about her personal life or current whereabouts following her disappearance from public view in mid-1097.
